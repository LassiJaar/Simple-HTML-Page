About me!

​I am a game developer from Finland, Northern Ostrobothnia. I am a graduate of OSAO, Kaukovainio's unit in Game Development.

Currently completing my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology at Oulu University of Applied Sciences.

I became a semi-professional Dungeon Master at the beginning of 2026, which has given me valuable skills in group leading, organizing, and team work.

I'm super interested in making loads of different AI and in neural networks, especially machine-learning/evolving AI and in simulations. (Learn more at the bottom of the Projects-page)

My first game project ever was back when I was maybe 10-years old, and was made either using Notepad or Scratch (Can't remember exactly which one came first).

What I lack in knowledge and skills, I make up in my willingness to learn, and my ability to do so.

Farmful

Farmful is once again one of my greatest projects yet! This was my first mobile game, but sadly it is only available for Android. 

This project taught me a great lot about many different things. The hardest things in this project were the idle mechanic, the ads and all the different values I had to save and load when the game is opened or closed. 

It was such an awesome feeling to run the app on my own phone for the first time. And I spent a great amount of time perfecting and balancing the game mechanics.

It was fun to make up and name all the different plants, and I see a good future with this project.

TRY THE GAME HERE! (Android Only)

Crazy Crash Rally Drive

Here comes my best school project yet! This game was showcased in Vectorama, and I was very proud of it. It was super rewarding to see people playing our game and enjoying it.

This was another group project, and I got added into the group a little bit after they had started making the game.

My contribution to this game mainly consisted of a 3D-model jerrycan, and the obstacle random-generation. I also made a sound manager for this project, but not many sounds were added to this game sadly. 

The point of the game is to just drive forward for as long as you can while collecting money boxes and trying not to run out of gas or collide with obstacles. This game is fun to play as a challenge with friends; see who can keep going for the longest!

TRY THE GAME HERE!

The Lost Sombrero

The Lost Sombrero is my greatest and most ambitious game project so far. It started as a school-project where I focused on a good story, but then I turned it into a personal challenge once again. This is the first project for which I have had any fans for, before it was even published.

So far, this has taken me the most amount of time of all my projects, and so this has taught me the most. Through this project I also learned a little bit about marketing.

Long story short; You have to help Pablo The Troll get back his sombrero which has been stolen from him by Hursti The Meetvursti. Pablo uses nachos as his main weapon.

This was also the first game which I added a randomly generated shop system. It was also a nice experience.
